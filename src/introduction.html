Peer to Peer Science: a new way of SenseMaking

INTRODUCTION

Science provides humans with the best tool for understanding and making decisions. The Scientific Method describes the rigorous approach where hypothesis are put to test in an experiment and then the results are put through a process we call peer review to provide some degree of rebustness to the truthfulness of the results. Dsensor builds on this legacy of wisdom and puts forward a basis to use computational consensus based on sensor data and a network hypothesis in the form of a computation.

SenseMaking: is the term used to describe the process of understanding the world around us based on science.
