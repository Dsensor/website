Peer to Peer Science: a new way of SenseMaking

Dsensor uses Peer to Peer technology to apply the scientific method to data collected from sensors. Traditionally, truth is established in science by experiment and the process of Peer review, Dsensor updates this to a computational consensus based on the Mapping Protocol. Dsensor is open-source; its design is public, nobody owns or controls Dsensor and everyone can take part in scientific inquiry. Everyone in the network is given the right to achieve science driven decisions.

Science helps us make sense of the world, our environment, climate, self and even the cosmos. SenseMaking is the term applied to this mission.
